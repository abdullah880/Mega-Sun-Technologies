Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about solar for homes

How much can I save with solar panels?

Most homeowners save 60–90% on their electricity bills. Your exact savings depend on:

  • Your current electricity consumption
  • Your roof's sun exposure
  • The size of your solar system
  • Local electricity rates and net metering policies

On average, our customers see a return on investment within 4-7 years, with 20+ years of free electricity afterwards.

How long does installation take?

From initial consultation to system activation, the process typically takes 4-8 weeks:

Once installed, most systems can be activated immediately after utility approval.

What happens during load shedding or power outages?

Standard On-Grid Systems:

Automatically shut down during outages for safety (as required by utility regulations). You won't have power during blackouts.

Hybrid Systems with Batteries:

Automatically switch to battery power during outages, keeping essential appliances running. You can design your system to power specific circuits or your entire home.

Is maintenance expensive?

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Here's what to expect:

  • Rain naturally cleans panels in most areas
  • Occasional cleaning (1-2 times per year) if you're in a dusty area
  • Inverter replacement may be needed after 10-15 years (warranty often covers this)
  • Monitoring system alerts you to any performance issues

Most maintenance is covered under our 10-year workmanship warranty, and panels typically come with 25-year performance warranties.
